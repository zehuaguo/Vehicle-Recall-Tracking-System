Manage Vehicle Recalls

The VRTS (Vehicle Recall Tracking System) allows easy maintenance of fleet informatin, campaign information and recall information. VRTS integrates with published VIN lists and manufacturer systems to seamless keep it's internal data up to date.

Our Tracking Platform

Driver/Custodian information

Manages the driver/custodian information for easy viewing, searching and reporting.

Campaign information

Manages campaign information of recalled ehicles for easy viewing, searching and reporting.

Campaign information

Manages data for all recall campaigns including recalled vehicles and status of recall.

Campaign Ratification

B2B interface to manufacturer to automatically update campaign ratification. Administrator provisions to perform a bulk update of campaign ratification information (via a manual file upload)

Recall notices

B2B interface to automatically update recall notices. Administrator provisions to perform a bulk upload of Recall Notices (via a manual file upload) (if B2B interface is not available to manufacturer or ACCC websites)

Automatic Vehicle Data Sync

Interface to automatically load vehicle data from all Eclipx Business Data sources into VRTS Database. Factor in business rules to identify vehicles which need to be monitored.

ACCC website interface

Interface to periodically read Recall campaigns and lists of affected VINs from ACCC website and identify the vehicles which are handled by Eclipx entities and require to be monitored.
